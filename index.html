Promenade PDX

▼

What is it?

PromenadePDX is an opportunity to pull out you fanciest attire and celebrate, dance and eat with Portland’s greatest (and fanciest) Individuals.

What do I wear?

PromenadePDX is all about self expression. We recommend not wearing anything that would fit more into the categories of costume and cosplay as we want to see your own unique style and your own personal definition of fancy Fashion.

When/Where is this happening?

This event will be four times a year weather permitting in the greater Portland area. More information to come!
